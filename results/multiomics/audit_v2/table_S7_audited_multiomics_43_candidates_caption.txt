Table S7. Audited transcriptomic, proteomic and phosphoproteomic evidence across the 43 candidate regulators.

One row per unique candidate from the frozen seed-excluded union of the GREmLN and GENIE3 top-25 rankings (GREmLN specific n=18, shared n=7, GENIE3 specific n=18), grouped and ordered by canonical model rank. Candidate mRNA is contextual evidence and is shown as two contrast-specific columns: TCR activation (TCR versus unstimulated control) and incremental BTLA (BTLA+TCR versus TCR). Protein abundance and phosphosite change (BTLA+TCR versus TCR) are independent observed molecular layers and define the independent molecular association call. Missing assay coverage is reported as “Absent from assay universe” and is not negative evidence.

Of 43 candidates, 41 were measured for mRNA (AHR and MSC absent); five were transcriptionally responsive to TCR activation (EGR2, HIVEP3, ID2, JUNB, PRDM1) and none showed a qualifying incremental BTLA mRNA response. Sixteen were measured for protein abundance (qualifying: SMAP2, STAT5B) and five for phosphosites (qualifying: GTF3C2, HIRIP3, MYC, STAU2, TFDP1). Seven candidates showed an independent molecular association (GTF3C2, HIRIP3, MYC, SMAP2, STAT5B, STAU2, TFDP1); none were shared candidates. Because the model rankings are unsigned, molecular changes are not labelled supportive or opposing. See footnotes a–h for thresholds and definitions.
