Figure | GENIE3 top 25 candidate regulator Paperclip evidence summary. Candidate regulators are ordered by their GENIE3 rank. Literature was retrieved using one fixed Paperclip query per candidate, "<TF> TCR inhibition", with the ten highest ranked results retained. Papers passed an entity identity gate before assessment by a fixed LLM judge and deterministic evidence rubric. The coloured tile shows the final usable evidence tier: strong, moderate, weak, none or missing. The five-number evidence string gives, from left to right, the numbers of papers returned, entity eligible, relevant, primary and directly causal or perturbational. A filled circle indicates at least one direct causal primary study; an open circle indicates none. Shared candidates also present in the GREmLN top 25 are marked with ●. GENIE3 contained 2 strong, 6 moderate, 6 weak, 10 no-evidence and 1 missing candidate tiers; 8 candidates had strong or moderate evidence. Paperclip supplied literature retrieval but did not assign the evidence tiers. Literature evidence was added after model ranking and was not a predictive input. The tier for NR4A2 remained missing because of a rubric validation failure (supporting_excerpt >20 words for PMC7883379); missing was not interpreted as no evidence. Human verification of strong and moderate key papers remained pending at the time of this audit.
